Welcome to the Tribute Page for Rohit Sharma

Rohit Sharma, fondly known as the "Hitman" of Indian cricket, is a name synonymous with elegance, power, and records. Born on April 30, 1987, in Bansod, Nagpur, Rohit's journey in cricket has been nothing short of spectacular.

Cricketing Achievements

Rohit made his debut for the Indian cricket team in 2007, and since then, he has become one of the leading run-scorers and most successful captains. Notably, Rohit is the only player to have scored three double centuries in ODIs. In 2019, he was appointed as vice-captain of the Indian cricket team in ODIs and T20Is, leading the team to numerous victories.

Indian Premier League (IPL)

Rohit Sharma is an iconic figure in the IPL, captaining the Mumbai Indians since 2013. Under his leadership, the team has secured multiple IPL titles, solidifying his reputation as one of the most successful captains in the league's history.

Records and Milestones

  • First Player to Score Three Double Centuries in ODIs: Against Australia, Sri Lanka, and West Indies.
  • Fastest T20I Century: Scored a century off just 35 balls against Sri Lanka in 2017.

Off the Field

Beyond the cricket field, Rohit Sharma is known for his philanthropy and sportsmanship. He actively supports various charitable causes and promotes cricket at the grassroots level.
